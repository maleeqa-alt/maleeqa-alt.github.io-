Top Tips for Teen Travellers

Always travel with a trusted adult or guardian, especially for international trips.

Keep important documents like passport, ID, and tickets in a secure place.

Learn a few basic phrases in the local language to help with communication.

Stay connected with family or friends regularly and share your itinerary.

Carry a portable phone charger because a dead phone can cause stress!

Be mindful of your belongings and never leave them unattended.

Respect local customs and traditions, it makes the experience richer.

Drink plenty of water and eat healthily to stay energized.

Plan your budget carefully and carry some emergency cash.

Research emergency contacts and local health services before you go.

Take photos, but also take time to enjoy moments without screens.

Have fun, stay safe, and be open to new adventures!
